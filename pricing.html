Materials & Prep

What You Need to Know

Materials are customer-provided. Our flat rate covers labor and standard tools only. You supply any materials, hardware, fixtures, or specialty items needed for your punch list.

Missing materials reduce your scope. If we arrive and materials aren't ready, we'll work on tasks we can complete. Time lost to material runs or waiting counts against your booked hours.

Prep matters. Clear work areas, accessible spaces, and organized materials mean we maximize your time block. The more prepared you are, the more we accomplish.
