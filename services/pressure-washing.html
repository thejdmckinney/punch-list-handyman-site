Pressure Washing

Professional pressure washing to restore curb appeal and remove years of dirt and grime.

What's Included

  • High-pressure cleaning of targeted surfaces
  • Appropriate pressure settings for each surface type
  • Removal of dirt, mildew, algae, and stains
  • Eco-friendly cleaning solutions when needed
  • Thorough rinse and spot treatment
  • Complete cleanup of debris

What We Clean

  • Driveways: Concrete and paver driveways
  • Patios & Decks: Concrete, stone, or composite surfaces
  • Walkways: Sidewalks and pathways
  • Fences: Wood and vinyl fencing
  • Siding: Vinyl, brick, and stucco exterior walls
  • Garage Floors: Oil stains and accumulated grime

Typical Time: 2–6 hours depending on area size

Best For: Spring cleaning, pre-listing prep, or annual maintenance. Dramatically improves curb appeal and can be combined with other outdoor tasks.
