Fence/Gate Repair

Fix sagging gates, replace damaged pickets, and reinforce fence sections for security and curb appeal.

What's Included

  • Picket and board replacement
  • Gate alignment and leveling
  • Hinge repair or replacement
  • Latch and lock installation
  • Post reinforcement or replacement
  • Structural bracing for sagging sections
  • Hardware upgrades (springs, closers, handles)

Common Repairs We Handle

  • Sagging Gates: Re-hanging, leveling, and diagonal bracing
  • Broken Pickets: Individual board replacement to match existing fence
  • Loose Posts: Reinforcement or concrete reset
  • Failed Hardware: New hinges, latches, springs, and closers
  • Storm Damage: Section repairs and panel replacement
  • Rotted Wood: Replacement of damaged sections

Typical Time: 2–6 hours depending on repair extent

Materials: You provide replacement boards and hardware, we handle all installation and adjustment work.
