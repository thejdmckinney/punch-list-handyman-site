Dryer Vent Cleaning

Thorough dryer vent cleaning to improve efficiency, reduce drying time, and prevent fire hazards.

What's Included

  • Complete lint removal from vent line
  • Cleaning of exterior vent hood
  • Inspection of vent connection and ducting
  • Airflow testing before and after cleaning
  • Identification of any vent damage or restrictions
  • Cleanup of all debris and lint

Why Regular Dryer Vent Cleaning Matters

  • Fire Prevention: Lint buildup is a leading cause of house fires
  • Energy Savings: Clean vents mean faster drying and lower energy bills
  • Extend Appliance Life: Reduce wear and tear on your dryer
  • Better Performance: Clothes dry faster and more evenly
  • Prevent Mold: Proper venting reduces moisture buildup

Typical Time: 45 minutes to 1.5 hours

Recommended: Annual cleaning for most households, more frequently for large families or heavy dryer use.
