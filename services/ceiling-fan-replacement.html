Ceiling Fan Replacement

Professional ceiling fan installation and removal. We handle everything from mounting to balancing and cleanup.

What's Included

  • Complete removal of old ceiling fan
  • Inspection of electrical box and reinforcement if needed
  • Installation of new fan with mounting hardware
  • Electrical connection and testing
  • Fan balancing for quiet operation
  • Cleanup and disposal of old fan

Important Details

What You Need to Provide: Your new ceiling fan (unboxed and ready)

Typical Time Required: 1–2 hours per fan

Perfect For: Bedrooms, living rooms, outdoor patios, and any space needing improved air circulation

Note: If your ceiling box isn't rated for fan support, we'll reinforce it during the visit. Vaulted or cathedral ceilings may require additional mounting hardware.
