Outlet & Switch Replacement

Upgrade to modern outlets and switches with safe, code-compliant installation.

What's Included

  • Safe removal of old outlets or switches
  • Electrical inspection and wire verification
  • Installation of new outlets or switches
  • Testing for proper function and grounding
  • Wall plate installation
  • Cleanup and disposal

Common Upgrades

  • GFCI Outlets: Required in bathrooms, kitchens, and outdoor areas
  • USB Outlets: Built-in USB charging ports for convenience
  • Dimmer Switches: Adjustable lighting control
  • Smart Switches: Wi-Fi enabled home automation switches
  • 3-Way Switches: Control lights from multiple locations
  • Tamper-Resistant Outlets: Child safety outlets

Typical Time: 15–30 minutes per outlet or switch

Note: You provide the outlets/switches, we handle the installation. We can install multiple units during a single visit to maximize efficiency.
