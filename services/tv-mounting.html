TV Mounting

Professional TV wall mounting with secure installation and clean wire management.

What's Included

  • Stud location and wall assessment
  • Secure bracket installation on wall
  • TV mounting to bracket with proper hardware
  • Leveling and viewing angle adjustment
  • Cable management and wire concealment
  • Connection testing and functionality check
  • Complete cleanup and packaging disposal

Mount Types & Options

  • Fixed Mounts: Slim, low-profile mounting flush to wall
  • Tilting Mounts: Vertical tilt adjustment for optimal viewing
  • Full-Motion Mounts: Swivel, extend, and tilt for maximum flexibility
  • Fireplace Mounting: Above-mantle installation with proper clearance
  • Wire Concealment: In-wall wire fishing or external cable covers
  • Soundbar Mounting: Coordinated mounting below TV

Typical Time: 1–2 hours per TV

What You Provide: TV, mount/bracket, and any cables. We bring the tools and expertise for safe, level installation.
