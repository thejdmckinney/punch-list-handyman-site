Drywall Repair

Expert drywall patching, crack repair, and texture matching to make walls look like new.

What's Included

  • Assessment of damaged area
  • Patching holes from small nail pops to larger damage
  • Crack filling and smoothing
  • Sanding and surface preparation
  • Texture matching (knockdown, orange peel, skip trowel)
  • Primer and paint application to blend with existing wall
  • Complete cleanup

Common Repairs We Handle

  • Nail Pops: Quick fixes for protruding nails
  • Small Holes: Picture hooks, doorknob damage, small punctures
  • Medium Holes: Fist-sized damage requiring backing support
  • Cracks: Settling cracks, corner cracks, seam separation
  • Water Damage: Ceiling stains, bubbling paint (after leak repair)
  • Texture Matching: Blending repairs seamlessly with existing wall finish

Typical Time: 1–3 hours depending on damage extent and drying time

Note: We can handle multiple repairs in one visit. Touch-up paint provided when possible, or we'll use your existing wall paint for perfect color matching.
