Door Repair/Installation

Fix alignment issues, upgrade hardware, or install new doors for improved function and appearance.

What's Included

  • Door alignment and hinge adjustment
  • Hardware replacement (handles, locks, hinges)
  • Weatherstripping installation or replacement
  • Strike plate adjustment for proper latching
  • Door trimming for clearance issues
  • New door installation (interior and exterior)
  • Door frame repair and reinforcement

Common Door Issues We Fix

  • Sticking Doors: Won't close properly or rubs on frame
  • Loose Hinges: Sagging doors or loose screws
  • Drafty Doors: Poor weatherstripping or gaps
  • Broken Hardware: Non-functioning locks, handles, or latches
  • Squeaky Hinges: Annoying noise when opening/closing
  • New Installations: Interior slab doors, pre-hung units, storm doors

Typical Time: 30 minutes to 3 hours depending on repair or installation

Note: For new door installations, you provide the door and hardware. We handle all the fitting, hanging, and finishing work.
