What Fits (and What Doesn't)

This is a one-visit service for small lingering fixes that never seem to get done. Clear scope keeps the promise simple.

What Fits Perfectly

Doors & Hardware

  • Door adjustments and realignment
  • Knob and handle replacements
  • Strike plate adjustments
  • Hinge tightening or replacement
  • Door stops and closers
  • Screen and storm door repairs

Walls, Trim & Finishes

  • Drywall patching and touch-up
  • Nail pops and screw pops
  • Baseboards and trim repairs
  • Caulking gaps and seams
  • Grout touch-up and resealing
  • Weatherstripping replacement

Small Installations

  • Picture and mirror hanging
  • Shelving and brackets
  • Curtain rods and blinds
  • Cabinet hardware
  • Towel bars and toilet paper holders
  • Simple furniture assembly

Everyday Annoyances

  • Squeaky floors or stairs
  • Loose railings
  • Sticking windows
  • Closet rod repairs
  • Drawer slide replacements
  • Minor deck and fence repairs

Sometimes Fits

These tasks fit if they're simple replacements and fit within your booked time block. No diagnostics, no troubleshooting, no multi-step processes.

Minor Electrical

  • Outlet and switch replacements
  • Light fixture swaps
  • Ceiling fan replacements
  • Dimmer switch installs

Minor Plumbing

  • Faucet replacements
  • Toilet hardware repairs
  • Showerhead swaps
  • Supply line replacements

Does Not Fit

These projects require licensed trades, permits, multi-day scheduling, or fall outside a punch list scope.

  • Remodeling or renovations
  • Structural modifications
  • Permit-required work
  • New electrical circuits or runs
  • New plumbing lines or drain work
  • HVAC installation or repair
  • Roofing or siding work
  • Window or door replacements
  • Full room painting
  • Flooring installation
  • Appliance repair or installation
  • Tree work or major landscaping
  • Foundation or crawl space work
  • Projects requiring return visits

Why These Limits Exist

These boundaries protect the one-visit promise. If a project requires multiple trips, specialized licensing, or uncertain scope, it doesn't fit the Knockout model.

We're not trying to be everything. We're trying to be excellent at one thing: showing up, working through your list, and finishing in a single focused visit.

Clear boundaries mean you know what to expect. No surprises. No scope creep. Just focused execution.

Still Unsure?

Make your list anyway. During booking, we'll review your punch list and confirm whether it fits. If something's borderline, we'll tell you upfront.

Better to ask than wonder. We'd rather set expectations clearly than show up unprepared.

Know What You Need?

Book your time block and send us your list. We'll confirm it fits and lock in your visit.
